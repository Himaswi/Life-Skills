## 1.  What is the Feynman Technique? (1 line)
- The Feynman Technique is a method where you learn something deeply by explaining it in simple words as if you are teaching someone else.

## 2. In this video, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?
- The most interesting idea was that anyone can learn any skill, even if they think they are not naturally good at it. Changing your learning strategies changes how well you learn.

## 3.  What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
- Active mode is when you focus and study with full attention.
- Diffused mode is when your mind relaxes, and ideas connect in the background.

## 4.  According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? Only mention the points.
Steps:
- Break the topic into smaller skills.
- Practice the most important parts first.
- Remove distractions.
- Practice for at least 20 hours.

## 5.  What are some of the actions you can take going forward to improve your learning process?
