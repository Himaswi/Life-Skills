## 1. What is Deep Work?
Deep Work means focusing completely on one important task without any distractions. It helps you learn faster and produce high-quality results in less time.

## 2. According to the author, how to do deep work properly? (Simple points)
- Choose a fixed time for deep work and follow it regularly.
- Remove all distractions like phone, social media, and noise.
- Work in a quiet place so your mind can stay fully focused.
- Take breaks in between to keep your mind fresh.

## 4. What are the dangers of social media? (in brief)
- It can distract you easily and reduce your focus on important work.
- Spending too much time online can make you feel stressed, anxious, or compare yourself with others.
- It can harm your sleep and productivity if used late at night or too often.
- It exposes you to fake news, misinformation, and unnecessary negative content.
